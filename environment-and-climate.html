Piccolina Environment and Climate

Committed to a Sustainable Future

Reducing Environmental Impact

At Piccolina, we are dedicated to minimizing the environmental impact of our operations. We prioritize the efficient and
responsible use of natural resources, investing in renewable energy and supporting the development of low-carbon technologies and fuels.

Climate Strategy

Our climate strategy focuses on reducing greenhouse gas emissions throughout our supply chains.
We aim to decarbonize our operations and work with partners to develop renewable and low-carbon energy solutions.

Pathway to Carbon Neutrality

Piccolina is committed to achieving carbon neutrality in our operations by 2050. We target a 50% reduction in operational emissions by 2032 t hrough measures such as renewable energy adoption, energy efficiency improvements, and the incorporation of low-carbon technologies..

Environmental Management

We implement robust environmental management practices to address biodiversity, water conservation, waste management, spill prevention, and air quality. Our policies ensure that we continually strive to reduce our environmental footprint.

Innovative Initiatives

From reducing emissions at our facilities to developing platforms for supply chain emissions data, Piccolina leads in creating sustainable solutions for the future.

LOCATIONS

We operate across a global network

Our business brings together an international team based in more than 50 locations worldwide. We work with producers and customers in over 150 countries.

RESPONSIBLY SUPPLYING RESOURCES

Our role in the energy transition

We play an active role in the energy transition, responsibly supplying vital commodities to meet current and future needs.
