Piccolina Oil and Petroleum Products

Fuelling Progress with Responsible Energy

Comprehensive Energy Solutions

Piccolina leads in the responsible supply of oil and petroleum products, including gasoline, diesel,
and jet fuel. Our global expertise ensures efficient and reliable energy delivery to meet diverse industrial needs.

Versatile Applications

Crude oil, a versatile raw material, produces essential products for daily life. Piccolina sources, stores,
and supplies a wide range of petroleum products, adhering to stringent environmental regulations to enhance performance.

Supply Chain Excellence

Our efficient supply chain strategies involve diverse transport methods and strategic storage locations, ensuring seamless delivery even to remote areas.
We specialize in delivering essential products to landlocked communities with innovative logistical solutions.

Alternative Fuels Initiative

In our commitment to sustainability, Piccolina is advancing the adoption of sustainable aviation fuel and biofuels, helping to decarbonize the aviation industry and offering low-carbon alternatives to traditional fuels.

LOCATIONS

We operate across a global network

Our business brings together an international team based in more than 50 locations worldwide. We work with producers and customers in over 150 countries.
